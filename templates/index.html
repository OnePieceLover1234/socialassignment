🌎 About The Mango Republic

We are a progressive, democratic micronation committed to sustainability, innovation, and social equality. We believe immigration strengthens economic growth, cultural diversity, and global cooperation.


Our government is liberal-democratic, prioritizing inclusion, fairness, and evidence-based policy.

📜 Act Objectives

  • Promote economic development through skilled immigration.
  • Protect refugees fleeing persecution.
  • Reunite families.
  • Maintain strict public health and safety.
  • Ensure fair, transparent, and ethical immigration decisions.

Our Legal Framework

How the C.H.U.D. Act structures our immigration system.

Domestic Foundation

A sovereign domestic statute. Value-driven and self-contained, prioritizing streamlined processing over bureaucracy.

Refugee Definition

Based on "credible fear" of persecution. We prioritize humanitarian urgency and allow flexibility in documentation.

Non-Refoulement

Absolute protection. No one is returned to face torture or death. This overrides deportation except in extreme security cases.

Screening

Includes international criminal checks, anti-extremism verification, and health screening (only excludes unmanageable risks).

Decision Body

Administrative Refugee Officers make decisions for speed. An independent appeals board ensures transparency.

Integration Rights

Immediate work rights, language training, and community mentorship upon approval.

Decision Making

📈

Economic Factors

Contribution to labor market, innovation, and reducing skill shortages.

🏛

Political Factors

Respect for democratic values and constitutional rights.

🏥

Health Factors

Screening for contagious diseases to protect public health.

🛡️

Security Factors

Criminal checks and protection against organized crime.

❌

Grounds for Refusal

Fraud/False documents, Serious criminal history, Active security threats, or Unmanageable public health risks.

🌱

Ethical Considerations

We balance compassion with security. We acknowledge that points systems can be strict, which is why humanitarian streams exist to ensure fairness.

Policy Defense (FAQ)

Why use a points system?
It creates objective, measurable standards that reduce bias and prioritize national needs.
How do you balance compassion and security?
Separate economic and refugee streams ensure humanitarian protection without compromising public safety.
Why include health screening?
To protect public health while still allowing treatment plans when possible.
How does this reflect liberal values?
It promotes diversity, equality, human rights, and evidence-based decision-making.
